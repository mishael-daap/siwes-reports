## Weekly Summary

This week was centered on the development and refinement of the Metaforce SaaS platform, with the primary focus being the construction and polish of the agent's landing page and interface. I built the core landing page that serves as the central entry point for the platform, integrating essential presentation components and establishing a cohesive visual foundation. A significant portion of the week's effort was dedicated to interface quality improvements, including the transition to a dark mode theme, systematic UI upgrades, and the development of a responsive FAQ section. These enhancements collectively elevated the platform's professional appearance and usability.

On the backend, I resolved critical stability issues by fixing errors within the agent system and implementing a ping-pong architecture to manage HTTP timeouts during agent actions. This pattern maintains persistent connections through periodic heartbeat exchanges, preventing session drops during long-running server processes and ensuring reliable communication between the client and server. Complementing the technical work, I established the company's LinkedIn presence and designed the organizational logo, contributing to the brand identity and external market positioning. I also continued structured learning through Trailhead modules, reinforcing Salesforce development competencies that directly support the platform's architecture. The week's breadth of work spanning frontend engineering, backend reliability, and brand development demonstrated a well-rounded progression in software engineering and product development capabilities.
