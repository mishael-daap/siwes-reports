## Weekly Summary

This week marked the beginning of my internship and was dedicated to comprehensive onboarding, team integration, and foundational learning across the Salesforce and AI agent domains. I was introduced to the company, my team, and my supervisor, and I began familiarizing myself with Salesforce, a cloud-based platform for managing customer relationships and business processes. The initial days focused on understanding the organizational structure, development workflows, and my specific role as the AI Strategist Lead, which was clarified through detailed one-on-one discussions with my supervisor, Mr. Shedrack.

A significant portion of the week was invested in structured learning through Salesforce's Trailhead platform. I followed the Agent Blazer trail, which provided an in-depth introduction to creating AI agents within the Salesforce ecosystem. This trail covered the architecture of automated systems that assist users and perform platform tasks within Salesforce, including how to configure agent capabilities, define action boundaries, and ensure safe and effective agent behavior. I completed the Agent Planning module, which was particularly valuable in establishing my understanding of AI agent design principles. The module covered critical topics such as risk management strategies for AI deployments, the distinction between deterministic and prompt-based agent actions, and the importance of mapping business processes to guide agent behavior in enterprise contexts.

I also set up my development environment, configured essential tools, and attended daily stand-up meetings to provide updates and discuss tasks with the team and my supervisor. On Friday, I visited the office to gain hands-on exposure to risk management and process mapping practices for AI agents, which complemented my Trailhead learning with real-world organizational context. Throughout the week, I actively participated in onboarding tasks and adapted quickly to the work environment, establishing a solid foundation for the technical and strategic work that would follow in subsequent weeks. The combination of structured learning, direct supervisor mentorship, and practical environment setup positioned me to contribute effectively to the Agent Blazor project and broader AI initiatives moving forward.
